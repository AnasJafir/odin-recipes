Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, and water in a large mixing bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix yeast and olive oil in a small bowl. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Add flour mixture to yeast mixture. Stir until dough comes together. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Scatter flour on bottom of a 9x13-inch pan.
  6. Brush with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
